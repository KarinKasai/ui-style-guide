grids

our grids

The grid structure is composed of a one column grid, a two column grid, a three column grid, and a four column grid. This grid is very flexible and can be changed depending on the content of any website. Although the grids can be used in a variety of ways, together, they help to unify content and create a sense of consistency through a website.

typography

our typography

Primary typeface

Ibarra Real Nova

Secondary typeface

Yantramanav

The Ibarra Real Nova typeface is best used for large and medium scale headlines. We will mostly use Regular and Semi Bold weight, along with a mixture of Italic style that creates sophisticated rythm. Yantramanav is mostly used for body copy. Note that there are specific letter spacing when it is used as headings or mini titles.

our navigation

Malleable global navigation includes a logo, navigation links, search bar, icons and a 'hamburger' menu button. Depending on width of screen for tablet (less than 650px wide) and mobile (less than 480px wide), the layout will be suppressed and only small version of logo and hamburger button are shown instead.
